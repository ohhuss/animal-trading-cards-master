Dogs

Gentle appearance.

  • Type: Lab
  • Avg Weight: 50 pounds
  • Avg Lifespan: 13 years
  • Habitat: Usually in a house

Dogs are worldwide pets who can be tamed easily (in most cases). They are born in litters that usually range anywhere from 3 to 12. There are many different breeds so it is hard to generalize, but they are a somewhat smart species of mammals.
